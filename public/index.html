FAQ

Why should I use Bite Club?
Support local businesses.

Buying from local restaurants instead of chain competitors recirculates three times as much money back into the community. We direct high-volume sales towards these businesses and increase their bottom line.

Get exclusively low prices.

We combine the sales volume of all large orders at a restaurant into one and unlock the highest discount possible for every participating customer. These exclusive discounts will save your tight budget.

Choose from a vast selection of restaurants.

We got you covered for every cuisine, date and time, delivery option, distance, and price range you could hope for.

How does Bite Club work?
  1. Fill out the form.
  2. Update the form when you have upcoming events. The more time you give us, the better your budget will feel.
  3. Recieve the best offer possible for your favorite restaurant.
  4. Select what menu items and quantities you would like to order, or let us take care of it for you.
  5. Make a payment to the restaurant and pick up your order.
